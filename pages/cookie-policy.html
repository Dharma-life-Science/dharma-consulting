1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function correctly, remember preferences, and collect analytical information about visitor behavior.

2. Cookies We Use

The Dharma Consulting website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled. They include session management and form security tokens.
  • Performance cookies: Anonymous analytics to understand how visitors use our website (page views, navigation paths). No personally identifiable information is collected through these cookies.

We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party marketing cookies.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to third-party services (LinkedIn, WhatsApp). These services have their own cookie policies, which we are not responsible for. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through our website.
